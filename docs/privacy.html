VS Brain — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30. Maintainer: Adam Wang (github.com/adamwang99/VS-Brain).

Short version: VS Brain is a Chrome side-panel extension that relays content between AI tabs you already have open. It runs locally in your browser. It does not have a server. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What VS Brain does on your machine

  • Reads the latest assistant reply from AI tabs you currently have open (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, Perplexity, Grok). It only reads pages on the explicit list of host permissions in the extension manifest.
  • Pastes that reply into the other open AI tab so the two AIs can critique each other.
  • Saves a final blueprint or decision-ledger bundle to your browser's Downloads folder when you click finalize.
  • Keeps small UI state (last selected mode, last entered prompt template, debug log lines) in chrome.storage.local on your own machine.

What VS Brain does not do

  • No server-side processing. The extension has no backend at this time. Nothing you type or paste is sent to a server controlled by us.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking. No third-party analytics SDKs are bundled.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data with anyone, anywhere.
  • No advertising.
  • No background access to tabs outside the host permissions listed in the manifest.

Permissions and why each is needed

  • activeTab, tabs, scripting: required to read the latest assistant reply from the AI tab you choose, and to paste critique into the destination AI tab.
  • storage: required to remember your selected mode, custom prompt template, and minor UI state across sessions on your own browser.
  • downloads: required to save the final blueprint / decision-ledger bundle as a file in your Downloads folder when you click finalize.
  • sidePanel: required because the entire UI is rendered as a Chrome side panel.
  • windows: required to bring an AI tab to the foreground when relaying a turn.
  • host_permissions: explicitly limited to the listed AI sites (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com, gemini.google.com, claude.ai, chat.deepseek.com, www.perplexity.ai, grok.com) plus a local 127.0.0.1 entry used only for the developer mock lab. The extension cannot read content on any other website.

Sensitive content reminder

Because the extension reads the latest assistant reply from your AI tab, that content stays on your computer and is sent to the other AI tab you yourself have open. Do not use VS Brain on conversations that contain content you would not paste yourself between two AI tools.

Account and login

VS Brain does not have its own account system. It uses your existing logged-in sessions in each AI tab. We never see, intercept, or store your AI provider credentials.

Data retention and deletion

All data lives only on your machine. To delete it, remove the extension via chrome://extensions and clear the saved files in your Downloads folder if you no longer want them.

Children

VS Brain is not directed at children under 13. Please use the extension only if your AI provider accounts (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) are themselves used in compliance with their respective age policies.

Changes to this policy

If the extension ever introduces server-side features (for example a paid tier with hosted prompts), this policy will be updated before that build ships, and the change will be linked from the same URL.
